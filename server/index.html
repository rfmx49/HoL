Welcome to Room Jume!

Room Jume is a HTML5 puzzle/maze game. Room Jume places you inside a maze of office rooms. Your objective is to visit as many offices as possible. The catch is you must return to your starting location.

Sounds easy enough right? Well this office building is no ordianry office building. One mis step can take you completly off route.

Participate in Daily Challanges and pit your wits and memory against other challangers.
